Linked Lists
    Lookup - O(n)
    Insert  
        At Beginning - O(1)
        At middle - O(n)
        At end - O(1)
    Delete
        From Beginning - O(1)
        From middle - O(n)
        From end - O(n)

    Peek - O(1)

Queue
    Enqueue - O(1)
    Dequeue - O(1)
    Peek - O(1)
    isEmpty - O(1)
    isFull - O(1)

    Insert - O(1)
